Welcome to Georgian History and Culture

Georgia is a country at the crossroads of Europe and Asia, known for its rich history and beautiful landscapes. This website explores various aspects of Georgian culture, history, and art.

The Origins of Georgia

Georgia has a history that dates back to ancient times. Archaeological evidence suggests that humans have inhabited Georgia since the early Stone Age.

The Ancient Kingdoms

In the first millennium BC, powerful kingdoms like Colchis and Iberia emerged. Colchis is famously linked to the legend of the Golden Fleece.

The Rise of Christianity

Georgia became one of the earliest countries to adopt Christianity, in the 4th century AD. This had a profound influence on Georgian culture and identity.

The Golden Age of Georgia

During the 11th and 12th centuries, under the reign of King David the Builder and Queen Tamar, Georgia experienced a Golden Age of political and cultural flourishing.


Modern Georgia

Today, Georgia is a rapidly developing nation with a blend of tradition and modernity.
